My Family

“People say I’m crazy. If they met the rest of my family they would understand why.”-Anonymous

I live with my mom, my dad, and my little sister, and we have two cats and a bearded dragon. My grandparents on both sides of the family are from all over Europe, including (but not limited to) Germany, Austria, France, England, and possibly Ireland. Every December, my dad’s uncle throws a Christmas party where most, if not all of our extended family is invited. So, I know my dad’s cousins and aunts and uncles much better than my mom’s, whom I’ve never met, yet my mom will claim I have. As of right now, my mom's uncle recently passed away, and while I barely knew him, it's unsettling to see the rest of my family so upset.
